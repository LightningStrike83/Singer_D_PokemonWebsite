About The Site

This site is dedicated for Pokemon fans, developed by Pokemon fans. This site is inspired by other fan-driven content creators and websites such as Pheonixmaster1 and Joe Merrick/Serebii.

The purpose of this website is to offer and compile information that may not be easily found or easily accessible, to help any trainer in need, as well as offer activities that may not be easily found across the web. The devs are passionate for Pokemon and wish to give back to the Pokemon community that has brought them joy and help in their times of need.

All sources of information presented in this website can be found here.

About The Devs

Lightning

Lightning has been an avid Pokemon fan, ever since she was young. Her first game was Pokemon Colosseum for the Gamecube, with Pokemon Emerald being her first mainline game. Pokemon has been her main source of inspiration, as well as one of the main sources of her strength. She has dabbled into many aspects of Pokemon, including competitive, shiny hunting, and creating art. She loves Pokemon for the potential the entire Pokemon world has, and the happiness it brings to others as well.

  • Favourite Pokemon: Eevee
  • First Shiny Pokemon: Magikarp (LeafGreen)
  • Favourite Trainer: Serena

Lightning's Champion Team

Flan

Flan has had a passion for Pokemon ever since her youth. Some of her most precious childhood memories involve the happiness that Pokemon has brought to her. She is a major fan of Hoenn, with Pokemon Emerald being her first Pokemon game. She is an artist at heart, and has created many breath-taking pieces, some of them even Pokemon themed.

  • Favourite Pokemon: Slowpoke, Bulbasaur, Gengar
  • First Shiny Pokemon: Tropius (Emerald)
  • Favourite Trainer: Acerola

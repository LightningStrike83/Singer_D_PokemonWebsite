Pokemon Guess Who

Instructions

Wanna play a round of Guess Who with your friends? Generate a set of Pokemon you like, then send the id code to your friend to generate the same list! Select your Pokemon from the drop down menu, and "mark off" any Pokemon by tapping on their image. Or you can use these boards for bingo if you wish! Can be done solo or with someone else!
Have fun trainers!
